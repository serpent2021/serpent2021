Privacy Policy for Testing Facebook Login

Effective Date: 18 December, 2024

At Testing Facebook Login, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal data you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the data that is necessary for Facebook Login. When you log in to our app using Facebook, we may access the following information from your Facebook account:

  • Name: Your Facebook profile name.
  • Email address: Your Facebook email address (if permission granted).
  • Profile picture: Your Facebook profile photo (if permission granted).

Please note that we do not store any sensitive personal data or information beyond what is necessary for user authentication.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information obtained from Facebook solely for the purpose of user authentication and personalization. This data helps us:

  • Identify and authenticate you when you log into our application.
  • Provide personalized content and features within the app.

We do not share, sell, or distribute your personal information to third parties.

3. Data Storage

As of now, we do not store any personal data permanently. The data retrieved from Facebook (such as name, email, and profile picture) is only used for the duration of your session. If we do store data in the future, it will be in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

4. Cookies

We may use cookies or similar technologies to improve your experience on our website or app. These cookies are used for user authentication and session management. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect your ability to use certain features of the app.

http://localhost:3000/delete-user-data

If you request deletion, we will promptly remove any data associated with your account, if applicable.

6. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you provide, including using encryption and secure connections. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data.

7. Third-Party Services
